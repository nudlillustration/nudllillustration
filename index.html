About

Jostein Aase Tønnessen


Nudl Illustration is my a freelance business located in Norway, Stavanger. I've drawing my ever since i was little and i've enjoyed being able to express myself creatively. Most of my illustrations are done in Photoshop because i enjoy painting freely with brushes, while focusing on direction, colors and textures. I'm not too concerned with maintaining a particular style, because whenever i approach a subject i like to pick style based on what i believe suits the subject best.

Feel free to contact me if you have any questions.
